THE REAL REASON YOU LOSE

Most ranked games are not lost in aim duels.
They are lost in moments of emotional instability.

You swing too early.

You spray for confirmation.

You overpeek after damage.

You ego-challenge.

You tilt after one mistake.

Mechanics expose mistakes.
Emotion creates them.
